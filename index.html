Team Spirit is

Team Spirit (TS) is an esports organization from Serbia that represents the CIS region in Counter-Strike 2. The team was founded by Nikita "Cheshir" Chukalin in 2015 in Russia, but later moved its operations to Belgrade, Serbia.

Achievements of Team Spirit in CS2

Victory at PGL Astana 2026.
The team won 512,000 USD.
30 gold, 22 silver, and 18 bronze medals from various tournaments.
A record-breaking streak of 12 consecutive wins (from 2024-12-05 to 2025-02-02).
The longest losing streak is 7 matches (from 2019-02-02 to 2019-03-06).
1st place at PGL Astana 2026, 2nd place
Intel Extreme Masters Rio 2026 (IEM Rio 2026),
3rd place at Intel Extreme Masters Kraków 2026 (IEM Kraków 2026),
StarLadder Shanghai Major 2025
.
